FCI2.csv
Codebook

-------------------------------

BACKGROUND

Each year, Team Marketing Report computes the Fan Cost Index for each team from the four major professional sports leagues in the United States.
-------------------------------
DATA

The dataset consist of the 2013/2014 data for 122 teams.

-------------------------------

VARIABLES

team:        Team name

fci:         The Fan Cost Index (FCI) comprises the prices of four (4) average-price tickets, two (2) small draft beers, four (4) small
             soft drinks, four (4) regular-size hot dogs, parking for one (1) car, two (2) game programs and two (2) least-expensive, 
             adult-size adjustable caps. Costs were determined by telephone calls with representatives of the teams, venues and 
             concessionaires. Identical questions were asked in all interviews.

league:      Average time spent on homework per week, in hours, for all subjects

year:        Season the data was collected for

stadium:     Name of the stadium

yearOpened:  The year the stadium opened

location:    City where the team plays

COL:         Cost of living index. Higher values indicate a higher cost of living. Data collected from http://www.areavibes.com/

livability:  Liveability index. Higher values indicate a more livable city. Data collected from http://www.areavibes.com/

-------------------------------
