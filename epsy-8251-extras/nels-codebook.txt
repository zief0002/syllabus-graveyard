BACKGROUND

During the spring of 1988, NCES initiated a longitudinal study (National Educational Longitudinal Study) of 8th-grade students attending 1,052 high schools across the fifty states and the District of Columbia. These data constitute a representative sample of the original 27,394 participants.

The data come from from the National Educational Longitudinal Study (NELS).
-------------------------------
DATA

The data consist of 100 students.

-------------------------------

VARIABLES

grades:     Eighth-grade students’ overall GPA in all subjects (on a 100-point scale)

pared:      The level of education of the students’ parent (in years of schooling) with the most education

hwork:      Average time spent on homework per week, in hours, for all subjects
